How to Bowl

  1. Pick one to three pins.
  2. Add the numbers on the pins.
  3. Match a ball to the last digit.
  4. You scored!
  5. No match? Try the next ball.

Example: Pick pins 6, 8 and 7. They sum to 21. The last digit of 21 is 1. Pick ball 1. You score three points!
